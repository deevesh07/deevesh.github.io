3 Types of Devices

Raspberry Pi 4

The Raspberry Pi 4 is a small single-board computer used for coding, IoT, and networking projects. It’s powerful, affordable, and ideal for students and developers.

Arduino Uno

The Arduino Uno is a microcontroller board commonly used for electronics projects, robotics, and automation systems. It’s easy to program and beginner-friendly.

Laptop / PC

A laptop or PC is used for software development, research, and documentation. It’s the main tool for writing code, testing applications, and managing projects.
